Our exhibitions

Biology and Medicine

The biology and medicine exhibition showcases the wonders of the tiny world, from micro-organisms to the human body. It explores the history of medicine and major breakthroughs, including penicillin discovery and the first heart transplant. Observing animals in the wild has also led to many medicinal discoveries

Robotics and AI

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.

Ecology

The museum’s ecology section highlights the impact of humans on the planet, including global warming, species extinction, and pollution. It also explores ways to create a sustainable future for the planet and focuses on positive contributions we can make.

Oceanology

Dive into the depths of the ocean with our new exhibition, ‘Oceanology’. Discover the secrets of the ocean and learn about the creatures that call it home. Explore the wonders of the ocean and learn about its importance to our planet's ecosystem.

Cosmology

Explore the wonders of our cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. Learn about the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and the early navigators who used the stars to travel by.

Evolution

Discover the origins of life on this planet and how the species we know today have evolved over time. Meet some of the creatures who didn’t survive, including our Woolly Mammoth, the Dodos, and a number of dinosaurs too. Learn about where we come from and how we got here.

This months special exhibition

The astronaut's corner

This month we're setting up a minor exhibit in honor of the aeronautics and the humans explorations in outer space. We've created our very own spacecraft cabin. Come check it out!

Spaceship Wonderland

Our science-club members have sent us loads of differet spaceship creations to display in this exhibition. Do you want to make a contribution? Visit our arts and crafts setup where you can choose between loads of different materials to make you very own aircraft.
