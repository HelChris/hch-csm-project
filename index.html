Explore with us

Meet Wooly the Mammoth

See the amazing skeleton of a mammoth, an ancient animal that lived thousands of years ago. The mammoth was like a giant elephant with long, curved tusks and a long trunk. Learn about the history, evolution, and extinction of the mammoth. Don’t miss it!

Robotics Workshop

Learn how to build and program your own robot with this workshop! You will use a laptop, a speaker, and a small robot to make it move, talk, and interact. You will also explore the science and engineering behind robotics. This workshop is fun, hands-on, and creative. Join us and become a robotics expert!

Virtual Reality Painting

Have you ever wondered what it would be like to paint in a virtual world? Choose brushes, colors, and effects to make 3D artworks. Learn and have fun with art, technology, and science. Try it and be an artist!

Biology and Medicine

The biology and medicine exhibition showcases the wonders of the tiny world, from micro-organisms to the human body. It explores the history of medicine and major breakthroughs, including penicillin discovery and the first heart transplant. Observing animals in the wild has also led to many medicinal discoveries

Robotics and AI

The information revolution is here and robotics and artificial intelligence are the science of the future. From useful home applications of AI to industrial uses of robotics, the future is here. You can even say hello and shake the hand of Rob the Robot.

Ecology

The museum’s ecology section highlights the impact of humans on the planet, including global warming, species extinction, and pollution. It also explores ways to create a sustainable future for the planet and focuses on positive contributions we can make.
